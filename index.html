I am Gary, a PhD focusing on diffusion models and multimodal large models.

My research is dedicated to exploring the boundaries of generative AI, turning noise into art and enabling text, images, video, and 3D to flow freely across different modalities.

In this digital era, I believe AI is not just a tool but also an extension of creativity. By continuously optimizing the diffusion process, I am building intelligent systems capable of understanding and creating multimodal content.

Directions

01

Diffusion 扩散模型

Conduct an in-depth study of the mathematical principles of diffusion processes, explore more efficient sampling algorithms and noise scheduling strategies, and strive to reduce diffusion time from infinity to the millisecond level. 深入研究扩散过程的数学原理，探索更高效的采样算法和噪声调度策略。

Generation / Sampling
02

MLLMs 多模态融合

Construct a unified representation space that enables free modality conversion in latent space, achieving true cross-modal understanding and generation. 构建统一的表示空间，让模态在潜空间中自由转换。

Text / Image / Video / 3D
03

Efficiency 高效推理

Through compression, quantization, and distillation, bring large models to edge devices—making AI more ubiquitous and practical. 通过模型压缩、量化和蒸馏，让大模型在边缘设备上运行。

Compression / Distillation

Treat algorithms as brushes and noise as pigment; probe the resonance between machine creativity and human aesthetics. 将算法视为画笔，噪声视为颜料，探索机器创造力与人类审美的共鸣。

01 —

Beyond Randomness: Understand the Order of the Noise in Diffusion

2025.11 · Gary, et al. · Under Review

This work challenges the conventional view that initial noise in text-driven diffusion generation is merely random. It reveals analyzable semantic patterns in noise and proposes a training-free Semantic Erasure-Injection process for modulating initial noise in text-to-content diffusion models.

DiffusionNoise OptimizationTraining-free
02 —

Break Stylistic Sophon: Are We Really Meant to Confine the Imagination in Style Transfer?

2025.05 · Gary, et al. · Under Review

This work introduces StyleWallfacer, a unified training and inference framework for high-quality style transfer and text-driven stylization. It addresses semantic drift, overfitting, color limitation, and reference-image constraints through semantic style injection and triple diffusion.

AI ArtStyle TransferDiffusion
03 —

SHSRD: Efficient Conditional Diffusion Model for Single Hyperspectral Image Superresolution

2025.03 · Gary, et al. · JSTARS 2025

This paper proposes SHSRD, an efficient conditional diffusion framework for single hyperspectral image superresolution. It uses spectral information injection and two-stage transfer learning to improve generalization on small HSI datasets.
